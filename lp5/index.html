Overview

The overview of lesson 5 was learning CSS transitions.


Goals of the assignment

The goal of this assignment was to implement many transitions into our sites. This could be from implementing properties such as the many different transition properties. Examples being, transition-timing-property or transition-duration, etc.

I learned how to use best practices to use them in my practice. The examples I did help me further understand how transitions work.


Reflection

Overall I didn't know much about transitions and don't think I ever used them before. The assessment was alright and I'd say I had an alright time doing this, not too hard and not simple either.
